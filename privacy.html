Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the CAVES Drone Synthesizer mobile application.

What We Collect

By default, CAVES collects no personal information. The app operates offline and all synthesizer settings are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to use optional patch sharing features, we may collect only the synthesizer patch data (parameter settings) that you explicitly choose to share with the community. No personal information is collected.

Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the app means you accept any changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub Issues page.
